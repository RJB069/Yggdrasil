Jotunheim is the "Realm of the Giants", it is separated from Asgard, and Midgard by a river and is thought to be a wild and chaotic place. It is one of the first worlds and an ancient land, where the giants live. The Aesir gods and giants are enemies, although there have been love affairs and the acceptance of Loki the god of mischief. The land of Jotunheim is covered in rocks, wilderness and dense forests it is thought that the giants survived by fishing and hunting, as the land was infertile.

The well of wisdom, Mimir, at the base of Yggdrisal's roots is located in Jotunhem. This indicates that the giants aren't all bad as they are the keepers of ancient knowledge.
