Yggdrasil is an Ash tree, that extends out over all of the 9 known realms and above the heavens.
The 3 Roots of Yggdrasil are:

The first root - Is in Asguard home of the Gods, and is next to the well name Urd and this is where the gods had daily meetings.
The second root - Goes to Jotunheim and is next to the Mimir's well.
The third root - Goes to Niflheim, the well close to it is Hvergelmir. The dragon Nidhug is chewing on this root. Nidhug will also feast on the blood of the dead bodies that arrive in Hel.

At the top of Yggdrasil there is a nameless eagle that is Nidhug are enemies, there is a squirrel name Ratatosk. The eagle stays at the top of the tree and Nidug at the bottom, Ratatosk is the reason that the hatred between Nidhug and the eagle continues to grow, every time one of the two says something about the other Ratatosk runs to tell the other.

A Yggdrasil may look like at tree but it is a series of different worm holes, gates and/or travel lanes between worlds, and galaxies.What Yggdrasil is really a map of the stars.
