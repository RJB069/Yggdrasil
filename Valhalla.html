Valhalla is where the Valkyre bring the honored dead, those how have fallen in combat. The only way to enter these hollowed halls was to die in battel, those that died of old age or disease were not permitted into Valhalla, but were sent to Helheim Realm of the Dishonored Dead.

The Halls of Valhalla are ruled by the all father Odin. Where the warriors live blissfully feasting on slaughtered roast boar, drinking liquor that flows from the udders of a goat, and fight battels with one another until the days of Ragnarok come.

Once the days of Ragnarok come, the honored dead with march out of the 540 doors of the palace, and fight by Odin's side against the gaints.
